About Me

What do you want to know ?

Anya, a clockmaker in a neon-drenched city, builds a moonlit clock for Cassian, a star-eyed stranger. Each tick of the clock unweaves his cloak, woven from stolen memory, threatening a slumbering god. Together they chase whispers across dreamscapes, Anya's clock their guide, to mend the stolen time. Facing the cosmic giant, Cassian reveals himself as the god's lost dream, yearning to rejoin existence. The clock chimes its final song, dissolving into moonlight, and the god smiles, the universe restored. Dawn breaks, the city humming with mended dreams, and Anya and Cassian, bound by stardust and whispers, walk onward, a gear held tight, a reminder of their dance with time.
